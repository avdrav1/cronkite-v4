# Product Overview

This is **Cronkite**, an AI-powered news aggregation and curation platform. The application provides users with a personalized feed of articles from various sources, enhanced with AI-driven features like trending topic clustering and intelligent content organization.

## Key Features

- **Personalized News Feed**: AI-curated articles based on user interests and reading patterns
- **Trending Topic Clusters**: Groups related articles into thematic clusters for better discovery
- **Smart Filtering**: Filter by read/unread status, starred articles, and specific sources
- **Article Management**: Star articles, mark as read, and remove unwanted content
- **Social Layer**: Connect with friends through mutual confirmation and engage through comments and tags
- **Friend Management**: Send, accept, and manage friend requests with two-way confirmation
- **Social Interactions**: Comment on articles and tag friends in discussions
- **Onboarding Flow**: Guided setup to customize user preferences and interests
- **Settings Management**: Control feed sources, appearance, user preferences, and social settings
- **Responsive Design**: Works seamlessly across desktop and mobile devices

## User Experience

The app follows a clean, minimalist design philosophy emphasizing content readability and user focus. The interface uses a masonry grid layout for optimal content presentation and includes smooth animations and transitions for enhanced user experience.

## Target Audience

News enthusiasts, professionals, and anyone seeking a curated, distraction-free news reading experience with AI-powered content discovery and meaningful social engagement around current events.
